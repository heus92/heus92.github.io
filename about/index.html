About Me 👋

My name is Rashad but you already knew that, didn’t you? Basically I’m a product addict. I love digging around into all sort of fun and incredible products I can find. From the ones I use everyday to my friends’ startup projects or even low tech random hardware from the 90s’ I find on the streets, every product has something to teach us so let’s find out!

I’m always eager to collaborate with new people on new (or old) subjects to deliver great value to people in the world. From youngster looking for a ride, to African migrants starting a new life in France everyone deserves a great product. Let’s build that together!

I’m available for all kind of product-related missions. I’m more about the outcome and the project than the title of being a product-whatever-manager or a data-bs-specialist. So if your project is about making the world a better place I’m waiting for you!
